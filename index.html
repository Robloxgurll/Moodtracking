What is Mental health

Welcome to our Mood Tracker and Journaling platform! This tool is designed to help you understand your emotions, reflect on your thoughts, and improve your mental well-being.

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being, affecting how they think, feel, and act. It influences how individuals handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions throughout their lives. Maintaining good mental health is just as important as physical health, as it contributes to overall well-being and quality of life
